Welcome to H-HEAS

The House-to-House Emergency Alert System (H-HEAS) is an innovative project developed by high school students from Santa Fe National High School to enhance emergency communication between households and local responders. The system is currently being tested exclusively in Barangay Balidbid, one of the barangays in the municipality of Santa Fe, serving as the pilot area to evaluate its effectiveness in real-time emergency situations. H-HEAS enables residents to send distress signals directly from their homes, which are received by a designated monitoring base. This ensures that emergency responders can react swiftly and efficiently, improving overall community safety. The project aims to establish a reliable and accessible emergency response system, particularly in areas where conventional communication methods may be slow or ineffective.
